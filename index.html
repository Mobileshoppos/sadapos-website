What is a Light ERP System?

A Light ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) goes far beyond standard POS billing. It is a complete retail management system that helps businesses manage invoices, CRM, accounting, and complex inventory like warranties, batches, and expiries from a single screen.

SadaPOS is a highly optimized, offline-first Light ERP. It combines fast checkout, advanced inventory tracking, customer ledgers, and detailed financial reports in one easy-to-use platform. It helps businesses in Pakistan automate sales and simplify daily operations without the complexity of heavy, traditional ERPs.

FAQs

FAQs on Light ERP & Retail Software

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, our support team is always happy to help.

What is SadaPOS Retail Software?
SadaPOS is an optimized, offline-first Light ERP and Retail Management System. It combines fast invoicing, real-time inventory tracking, customer ledgers, and advanced business reports into one easy-to-use platform.
Does it work offline without internet?
Yes, absolutely! SadaPOS is built with an offline-first architecture. It keeps your business running 24/7 during internet outages. You can create invoices, track stock, and print bills offline, and the system automatically syncs everything to the cloud when your connection is restored.
Can I track unique serial and IMEI numbers?
Yes. SadaPOS is engineered specifically for mobile, electronics, and appliance stores. You can track the complete lifecycle of every device via its unique IMEI or Serial number from purchase to sale, which prevents fraud and simplifies warranties.
Does SadaPOS support multi-counter billing?
Yes. You can run multiple billing counters in your store simultaneously. All sales, inventory changes, and invoices are synced in real-time across all devices to prevent stock mismatches.
Is there a free plan available?
Yes, SadaPOS offers a free-forever starter plan that includes core retail management features. You can use it to manage your store and upgrade to advanced plans only when your business scales.
What devices work with SadaPOS?
SadaPOS works on standard computers, laptops, and mobile devices. It is fully compatible with standard USB and Bluetooth barcode scanners, cash drawers, and 58mm/80mm thermal receipt printers.
