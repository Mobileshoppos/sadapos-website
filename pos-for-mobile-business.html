Mobile Shop FAQs

Common questions from mobile and electronics retailers.

How does IMEI tracking prevent theft and fraud?
Every time you purchase or sell a phone, its unique IMEI is recorded in SadaPOS. If a device goes missing or a customer tries to return a phone they didn't buy from you, you can instantly search the IMEI to verify its exact history in your store.
Can I accept split payments (e.g., half cash, half card)?
Yes! Mobile phones are expensive, and customers often pay using multiple methods. SadaPOS allows you to split a single bill into cash, credit card, or bank transfer easily at the checkout counter.
Can I manage used (second-hand) phones and repairs?
Absolutely. You can add used phones as unique inventory items with their specific purchasing costs. Additionally, our system lets you track repair jobs, parts used, and labor charges all in one place.
