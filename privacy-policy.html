Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SadaPOS (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, transfers, protects, stores, and processes your information when you use our Services. SadaPOS is committed to the privacy and secure processing of the personal data it maintains for its Customers in an open and transparent manner, in full compliance with international data protection standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Definitions

  • "Services" means the SadaPOS point of sale and inventory management platform, including the web application (app.sadapos.com), the website (sadapos.com), and any features or functionality provided by us.
  • "Customer" or "Merchant" means the person or entity who registers to use the Service. If you are using the Services on behalf of a business, you agree that you have the authority to bind that business to these terms.
  • "You" means the Customer and any authorized users (employees) under the Customer’s account.

2. Identifying the Data Controller and Data Processor

For the purposes of global privacy laws:

  • SadaPOS is the Controller for Customer (Merchant) Information.
  • SadaPOS is the Processor for Consumer (your customers) and Employee Information, where the Merchant is the Controller.

For any questions regarding your data, you may contact us at support@sadapos.com.

3. Types of Personal Data Collected

We collect and use several types of data, including:

  • Personal Data: Your first and last name, email address, business name, and telephone number.
  • Business Data: Information about products, inventory, pricing, sales, transactions, and taxes that you input into the system.
  • Consumer Information: Personal information relating to the consumers with whom you interact (your shop's customers), such as their names or phone numbers for invoicing.
  • Employee Information: Information relating to your staff members who use the system under your account.

4. How and What Information We Collect

  • Account Registration: We collect information when you create an account or modify your profile.
  • Operational Data: We store the information you upload (inventory, sales records, etc.).
  • Offline-First Functionality: To provide offline access, some data is stored locally on your device's secure browser storage and is synchronized with our cloud servers when an internet connection is available.
  • Payment Information: Our order process is conducted by our online reseller Paddle.com. Paddle is the Merchant of Record for all our orders. They collect and process your payment card details securely. SadaPOS does not store your full credit card information on its servers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information only where:

  • It is necessary to provide the Services to you.
  • It satisfies a legitimate interest (such as research, development, and security).
  • We need to comply with a legal obligation.
  • You have given us explicit consent.

6. Sharing & Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your data. We share information only as described below:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party partners for cloud hosting, infrastructure, and payment processing (Paddle). These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf.
  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose your information if required by law, governmental request, or to protect the safety and rights of our users.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@sadapos.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with Services. Even after cancellation, we may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. When we have no ongoing legitimate business need, we will either delete or anonymize your data.

9. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. All data transferred over the internet is encrypted using industry-standard TLS (HTTPS). We use secure cloud infrastructure to ensure your data is protected from unauthorized access.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data collection, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

11. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this webpage. Your continued use of the Services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
